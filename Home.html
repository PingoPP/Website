About Me

Hello, my name is Jaka Dacar. I am a student of Archaeology at the University of Ljubljana (I highly recommend studying in Ljubljana!). My main interest is combining technology with the past, and I found the perfect field for that – LiDAR analysis. My academic goal is to improve the methodology and understanding of archaeological landscapes in Slovenia.
